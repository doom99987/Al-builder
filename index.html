Getting Started
  1. 1
    Create an account
    Hit Register in the top-right. A username unlocks saving builds, leaderboards, trades, and parties.
  2. 2
    Plan your build
    Open the Builder, pick your race and classes, gear up, allocate mastery points, and check the Dmg Calculator.
  3. 3
    Train your QTEs
    Head to QTE Trainer and practice your weapon's mini-game. Set your ping offset for accurate simulation.
  4. 4
    Trade and party up
    Browse the market, post listings for items you need, and find a party to take on bosses together.
